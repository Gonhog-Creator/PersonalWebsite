Astrophotography

Since the age of fifteen, I have gazed up at the stars and wondered what they were. When I got my first camera, I pointed it up to the heavens and have gotten breathtaking views of the milky way. Recently I acquired a small telescope, allowing me to begin observation of deep space objects such as nebula, star clusters, and distant galaxies.
