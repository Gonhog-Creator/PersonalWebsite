Woodworking

I've been passionate about woodworking for as long as I can remember. There's something incredibly rewarding about taking a raw piece of wood—whether it's walnut, oak, purpleheart, zebrawood, or cedar—and transforming it into something both beautiful and useful. I create resin cutting boards, resin tables, as well as a range of furniture like benches, chairs, and other wooden art projects. Using tools like dremels, table saws, and band saws, I carefully craft each piece with a blend of traditional techniques and innovative design. Every project is a labor of love and I am constantly learning.
